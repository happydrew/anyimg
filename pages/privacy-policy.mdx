# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 13, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to AnyImg ("we," "our," or "us"), owned and operated by ZHU GE. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our AI image creation services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data

We may collect the following types of personal data:

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication information (such as from Google if you use SSO).
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our service, including prompts submitted, images generated, and feature usage patterns.
- **Payment Information**: When you purchase credits, we collect payment information, but we do not store complete payment details. These are processed by our secure payment processors.
- **Device Information**: Information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

### Non-Personal Data

We also collect non-personal data such as:
- Anonymous usage statistics
- Aggregated user behavior patterns
- Technical information for service improvement

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain our AI image generation services
- To process your payments and manage your credits
- To notify you about changes to our services
- To allow you to participate in interactive features
- To provide customer support
- To gather analysis to improve our services
- To monitor the usage of our services
- To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

- **Contract**: Processing necessary for the performance of our contract with you
- **Legitimate Interests**: Processing necessary for our legitimate interests
- **Consent**: Processing based on your consent
- **Legal Obligation**: Processing necessary to comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

## Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: The right to request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification**: The right to request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: The right to request deletion of your personal data
- **Restriction**: The right to request restriction of processing your data
- **Data Portability**: The right to request transfer of your data
- **Objection**: The right to object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [zhugetd@gmail.com](mailto:zhugetd@gmail.com).

### Prompts and Inputs

## Content Safety and Compliance

AnyImg is committed to responsible AI image generation. We have implemented the following measures:

### Content Moderation
- **Automated Filtering**: All image generation requests are automatically screened for inappropriate content.
- **Human Review**: When necessary, suspicious requests may undergo human review to ensure compliance.
- **Prohibited Content**: Our systems actively block requests that may result in explicit, violent, defamatory, or otherwise harmful content.

### Intellectual Property Protection
- **Preventative Measures**: Our AI models are designed to create original content rather than directly copying existing works.
- **Usage Restrictions**: We prohibit the use of our service to create content that infringes upon third-party intellectual property rights.
- **DMCA Compliance**: We promptly respond to valid takedown requests in accordance with applicable copyright laws.

### User Accountability
- We maintain records of image generation requests to ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.
- Users who repeatedly attempt to generate prohibited content may have their accounts suspended or terminated.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.
